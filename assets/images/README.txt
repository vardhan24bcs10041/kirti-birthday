IMAGES NEEDED FOR LANDING PAGE
================================

Place your images in this folder with these exact filenames:

1. kirti-photo.jpg (or .png)
   - Kirti's photo
   - Recommended size: 600x800px or similar portrait ratio
   - Will be displayed as the main focal point on the landing page

2. gingerale-bottle.jpg (or .png)
   - Photo of a ginger ale bottle
   - Recommended size: 200x400px or similar tall/bottle ratio
   - Will appear in the bottom-right area of the landing visual

3. strawberries.jpg (or .png)
   - Photo of strawberries
   - Recommended size: 300x300px or similar square ratio
   - Will appear in the top-left area of the landing visual

4. album-art.jpg (or .png)
   - Album art for the music player
   - Recommended size: 400x400px or square ratio
   - Will be displayed in the music player section
   - Can be a TV Girl album cover, custom design, or any image you like

NOTE: 
- If images are not found, the page will automatically fall back to CSS illustrations
- Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
- For best results, use high-quality images with good lighting
